FONTLOG for Sans Guilt Wafer Wafer

This file provides detailed information on the Sans Guilt Wafer Wafer font software.

Basic Font Information
--------------------------

The Sans Guilt Wafer font is the result of an wireframe style effect on Sans Guilt MB.
